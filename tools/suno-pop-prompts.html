Why Use a Pop-Specific Prompt Generator?

Pop music has distinct structural and production requirements. Generic prompts often miss key elements like catchy hooks, polished mixes, and radio-ready arrangements. This tool generates prompts specifically tuned for pop subgenres including synthpop, indie pop, dance pop, and K-pop.
