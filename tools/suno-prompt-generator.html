What is a Suno AI Prompt Generator?

A Suno AI prompt generator helps you create optimized text instructions for Suno's music AI. Instead of guessing what works, this tool structures your ideas into the exact format Suno understands: mood tags, BPM, instrument lists, and vocal types. Whether you need Suno prompts for vocals, hip hop beats, or cinematic scores, proper prompting is the difference between generic output and studio-quality tracks.

How to Use This Tool

  1. Select your Music Genre (e.g., Hip Hop, Synthwave, Jazz)
  2. Choose the Mood/Vibe that matches your vision
  3. Pick Main Instruments to feature in the track
  4. Set Vocal Type (Female, Male, Duet, or Instrumental)
  5. Adjust Tempo (BPM) slider to match desired energy
  6. Select Song Structure (Verse-Chorus, Loop, Full Song)
  7. Add Style Notes for specific production details
  8. Click Generate Prompt and copy to Suno Custom Mode

Frequently Asked Questions

How to write better prompts for Suno AI?

Be specific about production elements. Instead of "good song," use "polished studio mix, deep bass, crisp highs." Tag-based prompts consistently outperform natural language.

What is the structure of Suno prompts?

The optimal structure is: [Mood] [Genre], [BPM] BPM, [Instruments]. [Vocal Type]. [Structure]. [Style/Production Tags]. This mirrors how music metadata is organized.

How do I create better AI music?

Iterate on your prompts. Save successful combinations. Use our free prompts PDF as a starting point, then customize based on your genre needs.
